About

Bienvest Engineering was founded by Şevki Çelik, who graduated from ITU in 1984 with a master’s degree in mechanical engineering. After working for over 30 years at Mapa Otomotiv in roles including production manager, engineering manager, and executive board member, he established Bienvest Engineering to focus on international consultancy and project management.

Our fields of expertise include vehicle clutches, transmission NVH, and industrial plant projects and investment management. Şevki Çelik also serves as the general coordinator of Everestarge, a company specializing in aeronautics.
